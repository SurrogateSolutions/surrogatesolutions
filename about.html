About

A wise man once said, "everyone should have a website."

This is the mission of Surrogate Solutions: a one-man operation dedicated to this singular goal. Perhaps nostalgic for the bygone days of Web 1.0 and early Web 2.0, especially in these latter days of a bloated and glutted web, I believe that the ability to create and market simple, minimalist static web pages, ones that are as aesthetically pleasing as they are intuitive, is not only ambitious but radical.

The Mission

To deliver simple and cost efficiant web solutions with creative design and easy fuctionality, helping individuals, small businesses, and others establish an online presence.
